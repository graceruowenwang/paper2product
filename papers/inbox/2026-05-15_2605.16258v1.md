## 摘要

Reconstructing coherent 3D geometry and appearance from unposed multi-view images is a fundamental yet challenging problem in computer vision. Most existing visual geometry foundation models predict explicit geometry by regressing pixel-aligned pointmaps, often suffering from redundancy and limited geometric continuity. We propose IVGT, an Implicit Visual Geometry Transformer that implicitly models continuous and coherent geometry from pose-free multi-view images.
